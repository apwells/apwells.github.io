Hello!

Im Alex, nice to meet you

I'm a award winning music producer and composer born in Brighton, but now living in South London. I've spent the last 15 years writing and recording my own music and that of others. I was awarded a platinum record for my work with Rizzle Kicks, and then worked extensively with Hobbie Stuart and more pop singers. My own music is more Bowie/Talking heads, but i enjoy creating all sorts.

This background in pop and songwriting set me up for an unintentional career move into film and TV in 2016. Last year I worked on two feature films directed by Jason Wingard and composed by Dan Baboulene. The first "In Another Life" won Best UK Feature at Raindance, and the second "Eaten By Lions" is being released in cinemas March 2019. Dan and I also made the music for "Only Child" which was nominated for a Welsh Bafta.

I've since worked extensively for TV, film, and online with many different directors and producers. I work in any genre, ranging from electronic, pop, punk, and rock through to orchestral score. I love to add real instruments to my compositions and will always strive for the most characterful music.

I have an unhealthy addiction to trying new things, if you'd like to become an enabler in this, please drop me an email; I love working on new projects!
