Music Production & Composition

Age UK TV Ad - Lonely In A Crowd

Music Composer / Music Producer

This piece is currently running on TV. Directed by Michael Ferns and produced by Royle Productions in London. I wanted to create something subtle and beautiful that didn't lead emotionally too much. The narrator is telling his real story which is very moving, I didn't want to crowd that out with overly emotional music.

Feature Film - In Another Life

Music Producer

Composed by Dan Baboulene and directed by Jason Wingard. Winner of 2017 Raindance "Best UK Feature" award. A highly atmospheric score for this film set mainly in the Calais Jungle. A moving story following refugee Adnan as he tries to cross the channel to reunite with his wife.

Aperol Sprtiz - We Are Aperol People

Feature Film - Eaten By Lions

Music Producer

Composed by Dan Baboulene and directed by Jason Wingard. This upcoming feature is a hilarious comedy about Pete and Omar, two orphaned brothers who go in search of Omar's estranged father. We created a huge amount of music for this one. We flew to Prague to record the orchestra at Smecky Studios.

Grants Whisky - Shape Of Three

Music Composer / Music Producer / Sound Mixer / Sound Designer

Directed by Michael Ferns and produced by Lobster Creative. A really nice piece explaining the process of creating Grants Whisky with three types of wood. The linked version is just the short 15 second cut, but there are also 50 and 90 second versions which will be available soon.

Good Mornin'

Music Producer / Sound Design & Mixing

This was a promotional piece to raise awareness of an issue that is severely under-represented and close to our directors heart. We wanted to shock the audience, so the re-make of "Good Mornin'" but with a dark twist felt perfect.

Natwest - We Can Do This

Music Composer / Music Producer / Sound Designer / Sound Mixer

I was asked by Pretzel Films to try my hand at creating a more classical and filmic score for this piece. I created what is quite a simple, but emotive piece that I believe fits the film and follows the mood and key moments. The sound design involved re-creating all poolside sounds. My soundtrack won Highly Commended at the 2017 EVCOM awards.

Sound Design & Mixing

Kindness - World Kindness Day

Sound Mixer / Additional Composition

This lovely piece produced by Jungle Creations for VT has had a 3.7 million views just 2 days after release at the time of writing. It's a lovely message and has been very well received. The work for me was just a simple case of mixing the dialogue for clarity (as there were varying levels of quality of the recordings sent in). I also added some sound design and extra strings to the ending of the library track to give a little more emotion at that point.
